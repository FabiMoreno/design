What is a pattern library?

The Design Pattern Library contains the building blocks of a strong visual foundation.

If you're looking for our brand guidelines then you should be looking here.

Why a design pattern library?

We have a design pattern library to accomplish the following goals:

  • Foster cross department collaboration.
  • Create consistency in how we build and design Duo products.
  • Allow our designers and engineers to spend more time crafting delightful and thoughtful experiences for users.
  • Encourage the use of reusable code through patterns.
  • Get all of our patterns into one place to help identify and solve problem areas.

If you have any questions, feedback, or ideas you can contact us at dpl[at]duo.com
