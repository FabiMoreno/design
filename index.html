Design @ Duo

What is a pattern library

For Duo it's building blocks of a strong visual foundation, their matching code, use cases, and best practices.

If you're looking for our brand guidelines then you should be looking here.

Why a design pattern library?

We do this so the pattern library can become a living breathing document that serves it's users by accomplishing the following goals:

  • Fostering cross department collaboration.
  • Creating consistency in how we build and design Duo products.
  • Allowing our designers and engineers to spend more time crafting delightful and thoughtful experiences for users.
  • Encouraging the use of the patterns reusable code.
  • Getting all our patterns into one place to help identify and solve problem areas.

Now we're certainly not the first to do this and a lot of the principles we employee have been pioneered by others though we believe them to be best practices. Though we intend our pattern library to evolve and grow to fill the needs of it's users as the company grows in step.

If you have any questions, feedback, or ideas you can contact us at dpl[at]duo.com
